Craigslist Location Selection Redesign: Human Computer Interaction Study

I conducted this project over the course of a semester in my Human Computer Interaction class at Georgia Tech. In this study, the interface of Craigslist's location selection is redesigned to improve the usability for real users. The final prototype was a proof-of-concept dropdown search menu that was verified through an empirical evaluation.
