Dinner Party

Keywords: third party API, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Cookie Sessions, Postgres, knex, Docker

While at Capital One, I participated in the Capital One Developer Academy. As our Unit 2 project, we were required to work with a partner to create a fullstack application. In this project, my partner and I worked together to create a social media site called Dinner Party.

This site enabled users to create login accounts, login, and save and share recipes to an overall user board. The user could also search for recipes with the Spoonacular API, where they could search by ingredients or by recipe name. Then, they could view the instructions, ingredients, image of the dish, as well as any user comments and ratings at the bottom of the page and the average rating of the recipe.

This site was developed at Capital One with their resources so the source code and demo is private.
