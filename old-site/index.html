Hi! My name is Anne Noonan

I am a software engineer with a background in Chemical and Biomedical Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University and I am currently pursuing my Masters in Computer Science part-time at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I work at Capital One in Card Tech, where I am on a team that is creating a posted transactions data pipeline using managed EMR and Spark and developing cloud-native REST APIs in Golang as an abstraction to the financial core of the business.

I predominantly work on the backend of applications and cloud infrastructure (AWS) so making this website was a challenge for me. My personal passions in technology are modeling and analyzing complex processes and phenomena, such as taking something super complex like chemistry, biology, or health data and using computer science to study and model these complexities. I am also a huge advocate for ethical computing and seek to learn more each day on how we can use software engineering to create more equitable outcomes for all people. From my projects page, however, you can see I dabble in a lot of different projects and like to have fun!
